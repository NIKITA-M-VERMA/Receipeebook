#  ChowMein with Paneer
<img src ="https://cdn.dotpe.in/longtail/store-items/5815806/xNyL19qa.jpeg" width="550px" height="400px">




## Ingredients
- 8 oz chow mein noodles
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 cup diced paneer
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 green bell pepper, sliced
- 1 cup snow peas
- 1 cup sliced mushrooms
- 1 cup shredded carrots
- 1 tbsp grated ginger
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Cook the chow mein noodles according to package instructions.
2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Add the diced paneer and stir-fry until golden brown.
4. Add the sliced red and green bell peppers, snow peas, mushrooms, shredded carrots, grated ginger, and minced garlic. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
5. Add the cooked chow mein noodles to the skillet and toss to combine.
6. Add the soy sauce, oyster sauce, salt, and pepper to taste. Toss to combine.
7. Serve hot.

Enjoy your delicious Five Vegetable Chow Mein with Paneer!

#  Pizza Loaded with Cheese and Paneer

<img src ="https://www.archanaskitchen.com/images/archanaskitchen/0-Archanas-Kitchen-Recipes/2019/Roasted_Garlic_Pineapple_Paneer_Pizza_Video_Recipe_Britannia_Cheesy_Kitchen_9_1600.jpg" width="500px" height="400px">

## Ingredients
- 1 pizza crust
- 1/2 cup pizza sauce
- 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1 cup diced paneer
- 1/2 cup sliced black olives
- 1/2 cup sliced red onions
- 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
- 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
- 1/2 cup chopped spinach
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 425째F (218째C).
2. Place the pizza crust on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the crust.
4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
5. Add the diced paneer, sliced black olives, sliced red onions, sliced bell peppers, sliced mushrooms, and chopped spinach on top of the cheese.
6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
7. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
8. Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

# Spaghetti Carbonara

<img src ="https://encrypted-tbn0.gstatic.com/images?q=tbn:ANd9GcRogFnv6krfWsi-BzPmM1IH-ZcBpHfnX_X1oA&usqp=CAU" width="550px" height="400px">

## Ingredients
- 1 lb spaghetti
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 4 oz pancetta, diced
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
2. While the spaghetti is cooking, cook the pancetta in a large skillet over medium heat until crispy.
3. In a medium bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, Parmesan cheese, and heavy cream.
4. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the skillet with the pancetta. Toss to combine.
5. Remove the skillet from the heat and let it cool for 1-2 minutes.
6. Add the egg mixture to the skillet and toss to combine.
7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
8. Serve immediately.

Enjoy your delicious Spaghetti Carbonara!

# Chocolate Chip Cookies

<img src ="https://butternutbakeryblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/chocolate-chip-cookies.jpg" width="450" height="500px">

## Ingredients
- 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
- 3/4 cup white sugar
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 375째F (190째C).
2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
3. In a large bowl, beat together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until creamy.
4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
5. Gradually add the flour mixture to the butter mixture and mix until just combined.
6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto an ungreased baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
8. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
9. Remove from the oven and let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 2 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Enjoy your warm and chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies!
